Upload an image — or describe what you are looking for — to find similar items inside this collection.

About this search

This search finds the most visually similar items in the collection using embeddings. You can search two ways: upload a photo, scan, or screenshot to find look-alike images, or type a short description to find images matching your words.

The search runs entirely in your web browser: it computes a "fingerprint" of your image or text and compares it against pre-computed fingerprints for every item in this collection. Your image or text is never uploaded — it stays on your device.

Please note: Starting a search downloads the analysis model and collection index to your browser. This happens once; your browser caches the files for future visits.
