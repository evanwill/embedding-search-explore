About this search

Have a printer's mark you can't identify? Upload a photo, scan, or screenshot of it and this page will show you the most visually similar marks in the collection, with their metadata — a quick way to put a name to an unknown mark, or to find related marks worth comparing by eye.

The search runs entirely in your web browser: it computes a compact visual "fingerprint" of your image and compares it against pre-computed fingerprints for every mark in the collection. Your image is never uploaded — it stays on your device.

Starting the search downloads the image-analysis model and collection index to your browser. This happens once; your browser caches the files for future visits.
