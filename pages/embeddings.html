About this search

Select a photo, scan, or screenshot and this search will find the most visually similar marks in the collection using embeddings.

The search runs entirely in your web browser: it computes a visual "fingerprint" of your image and compares it against pre-computed fingerprints for every item in this collection. Your image is never uploaded — it stays on your device.

Please note: Starting the search downloads the image-analysis model and collection index to your browser. This happens once; your browser caches the files for future visits.
